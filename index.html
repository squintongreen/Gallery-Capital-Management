Navigating the Insurance Marketplace

Company

Gallery Capital Management, LLC is a limited liability company located in London, England. Founded in 2012 by John C Head III, Gallery Capital Management leverages the considerable experience of our managing directors by actively participating in the Lloyd’s of London insurance marketplace.

An alternative asset manager offering investors cost-effective access to a wide spectrum of insurance risks through the world renowned Lloyd’s of London insurance market, a 42.6 billion USD market.

Providing access to a marketplace with high barriers to entry that has little correlation to traditional investments such as equity and fixed income markets.

Designed to profit from the diversification that the Lloyd’s marketplace offers, the majority of risks underwritten in the market are non- catastrophe risks.

Senior management has more than 50 years’ experience of the Lloyd’s marketplace as well as the broader insurance industry, having sat on the boards of multi-billion dollar global insurance companies such as PartnerRe and Argenta Private Capital.

Marketplace

Lloyd's

Unlike many other insurance brands, Lloyd's is not a company; it's a market where our members join together as syndicates to insure risk. When we talk of Lloyd’s, we’re really referring to two distinct parts. The market, which is made up of many independent businesses, and the Corporation of Lloyd’s, which is there – broadly speaking – to oversee that market. These parts are distinct, but far from independent. Both work closely to maintain high standards of performance across the market.

The Marketplace

The Lloyd’s market is home to 57 managing agents and 94 syndicates, which offer an unrivalled concentration of specialist underwriting expertise and talent. Business at Lloyd’s is still conducted face-to-face, and the bustling Underwriting Room is central to the smooth running of the market. The market structure encourages innovation, speed and better value, making it attractive to policyholders and participants alike. Immediate access to decision-makers means that answers on whether a risk can be placed are made quickly, enabling the broker to provide fast, good-value solutions.

Management

John C Head III

John C Head III serves as the President and Co- Chief Investment Officer of Gallery Capital Management. He is responsible for developing Gallery’s investment strategy and the overall management of the company. Mr. Head has been a Name at Lloyd’s since 1982 and presently underwrites £4 million of capacity. Since 1985, Mr. Head has been Managing Member of Head & Co, an investment firm focused on insurance and insurance service companies. Prior to the establishment of Head & Co., Mr. Head was a Managing Director at Morgan Stanley & Co.
